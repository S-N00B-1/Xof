Special Containment Procedures:SCP-24156 is to be contained on an air gapped computer system in a standard containment vault. Anomalous intances of SCP-24156 are to be deleted from external databases, and all recent accessors of SCP-24156's vault are to be questioned for their use of the system.

Description: SCP-24156 is a series of anomalous images, files in the graphics interchange format, and videos; of a spinning minecraft fox. SCP-24156 is occasionally accomponied by a highly compressed version of the song "Funky Town" by "Lips, Inc.", or an equivalant cover.

Person(s) exposed to SCP-24156 are extremly entranced with the instance. In some circumstances person(s) exposed to SCP-24156 will feel an intense sensation of humour and dispair, these effects have been observed to persist for a maximum duration of 2 hours. During the period they (persons exposed) may also feel strongly compelled to spread instanced of SCP-24156 to others.

Discovery: Initial reports of SCP-24156 were located by an agent on The Discord platform on ██-██-████. (See Addendum-24156-A) SCP-24156 was orignally given disruption class VLAM, it was subsequently reclassified on 02-10-2022, due to reports that it had expanded to the Youtube plaform. (See Addendum-24156-B)

Addendum-24156-B: Discription of sighting.

The attached image (Fig A.) is a screen capture of a video titled "fox" an instance of SCP-24156 which was avalible to the general public until 02-10-2022, when the disruption class was increased. In the video a standard instance of SCP-24156 can be seen, although the quality of the video was heavily compressed.

An Instance SCP-24156 (Fig B.)

Addendum-24156-C: Description of sighting.

The attached image (Fig B.) is a screenshot from the video game 'Minecraft' in which an instance of SCP-24156 has been created on a public server. This instance was located by Agent ███████████ after leaving the intended bounds of the servers world. This specific instance of SCP-24156 is located in an area that appears to resemble a room from SCP-███ (also known as ███ ████████) but has been tested to not be an instance of SCP-███ due to its conventional geometry. (Further details can be found in the file of SCP-███.) This specific instance also lacks any version of the song "Funky Town."

Addendum-24156-D: Proposed addition.

This proposal moves to include another instance in SCP-24156's file, hereafter refered to as SCP-24156-B. SCP-24156-B is/may be an arctic specific colouration of SCP-24156. (Renamed under the proposal as SCP-24156-A.) The logic being that because SCP-24156-A's appearance resembles one of two possible colours in the video game "Minecraft" the addition would be a preemptive measure, in the event that SCP-24156 is not limited to only one of the colour variations.
